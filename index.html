Why Our 1" Diff Drop Kit Is a Must for Toyota Tacoma Kits

Lifting your Tacoma improves ground clearance and approach angles, but it also increases the strain on front CV axles due to sharper operating angles. Our diff drop kit corrects that by lowering the front differential by one inch, reducing CV stress and allowing smoother, more reliable drivetrain performance. Whether you’re running long-travel suspension, daily driving with a lift, or tackling technical trails, this kit helps preserve vital components and extends their lifespan. It’s a small part that delivers a big difference where it counts underneath your rig.

Prevent CV Damage Early

We developed this kit to address a common issue in lifted Tacomas: excessive CV axle angles that lead to binding, clicking, and even catastrophic failure. When combined with long‑travel suspension systems, extended coilovers, or aftermarket control arms, the factory differential position no longer supports proper axle alignment. That’s where our 1" Diff Drop Kit comes in.

If you’re using a 2.5" lift or bigger, or running aftermarket upper and lower control arms, dropping the front differential becomes essential not just for smoother operation but for long-term drivetrain health. Our kit lowers the diff just enough to relieve tension on the CVs without compromising ground clearance or suspension geometry.

This is one of those rare upgrades that’s both affordable and impactful. It doesn’t just prevent binding and vibration; it actively preserves your investment in expensive suspension components and keeps your rig trail-ready for the long haul. We consider it a foundational mod for any high-clearance or off-road Tacoma build especially before heading into more advanced suspension setups.
